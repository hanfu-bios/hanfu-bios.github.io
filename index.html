About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Biostatistics at the Ohio State University, advised by Dr. Kellie J. Archer. My thesis topic is high-dimensional feature selection for ordinal data and event-to-time data. Before this, I obtained my Master's degree in Biostatistics at the University of Michigan.
